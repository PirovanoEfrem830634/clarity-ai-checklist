CLARITY AI

Checklist for medical AI research

The CLARITY evaluation checklist is designed to provide clear and systematic guidance for assessing studies that utilize Artificial Intelligence (AI) in the medical field. This tool facilitates a comprehensive and objective evaluation of AI-based research, ensuring that all critical aspects of study design, implementation, and reporting are thoroughly examined.

Each section of the CLARITY evaluation file addresses a specific element of the study, from dataset design and data handling to results interpretation and ethical considerations. Users are guided through detailed questions designed to elicit key information and insights about the rigor, transparency, and clinical relevance of the study.

To ensure a standardized and transparent assessment process, each question can be rated on a scale of 0 to 5 stars, with 0 stars indicating lack of adherence and 5 stars representing exemplary compliance. This rating system enables a nuanced, structured, and fair evaluation of AI research in medicine.

Star rating scale

  • 0 stars — The study doesn’t meet the criteria or lacks crucial information.
  • 1 star — The study minimally meets the criteria or lacks crucial information.
  • 2 stars — The study meets some of the criteria but has significant weaknesses.
  • 3 stars — The study adequately meets the criteria with minor issues.
  • 4 stars — The study meets the criteria well with only minor improvements needed.
  • 5 stars — The study fully meets the criteria and represents best practice.
